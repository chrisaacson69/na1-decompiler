What it does

War is a subsystem entry point, not a formula. The driver ($9850) validates the target, debits the marching army, plays the animation, then transfers control to the bank-2 tactical combat engine (battle_init_driver $AFE1) where damage, morale, pursuit and the commander-resolution loop play out over up to 31 in-battle days.
